Blue Ridge Cemetery

About the Cemetery

Blue Ridge Cemetery is a peaceful and historic resting place nestled in the heart of Blue Ridge, Texas. With roots going back generations, the grounds are maintained with care and reverence by a board of directors and members of the Blue Ridge community.

Location

The cemetery stretches between State Highway 78 and Business 78 and sits on the North side of FM 981.

Burial Information

Burial plots may be available. Contact the cemetery administrator for more information on availability and guidelines.
