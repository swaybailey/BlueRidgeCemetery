History

The Blue Ridge Cemetery has served the community since the late 1800s, offering a peaceful resting place for generations of families in the Blue Ridge area.

Burial Information

Burial plots are currently available for purchase. Please refer to the contact section below for assistance and more information.

Funding

The Blue Ridge Cemetery is not owned or funded by the city. It is maintained by the generous funding of people in the community.

Once a year on the first Sunday in May, the cemetery association meets to elect board members, discuss any new or old business, as well as view the financial information of the cemetery. That weekend is Decoration Weekend.

Donations can be mailed to:
