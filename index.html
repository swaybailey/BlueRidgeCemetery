Blue Ridge Cemetery

About the Cemetery

Blue Ridge Cemetery is a peaceful and historic resting place nestled in the countryside of Blue Ridge, Texas. With roots going back generations, the grounds are maintained with care and reverence by the local community.

Location

Located just outside Blue Ridge, TX, off County Road 123. Open to visitors during daylight hours.
