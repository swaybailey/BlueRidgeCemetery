Funding

The Blue Ridge Cemetery is not owned or funded by the city. It is lovingly maintained by the generous support of individuals in the community.

Each year, on the first Sunday in May, the Cemetery Association meets to elect board members, discuss new and old business, and review the cemetery's financial information. This weekend is also known as Decoration Weekend.

Donations can be mailed to:
Blue Ridge Cemetery Incorporated
PO Box 122
Blue Ridge, TX 75424

Starting in 2025, First Baptist Church, located less than a quarter mile from the cemetery, has graciously offered to receive online donations on behalf of the cemetery at no cost.

"We are happy to help the cemetery by offering our website as a portal for online giving. We are already set up, and those donations can now be marked specifically for the cemetery in our giving portal.
