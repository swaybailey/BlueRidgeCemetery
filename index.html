Blue Ridge Cemetery

About the Cemetery

Blue Ridge Cemetery is a peaceful and historic resting place nestled in the heart of Blue Ridge, Texas. With roots going back generations, the grounds are maintained with care and reverence by a board of directors and members of the Blue Ridge community.

Location

The cemetery stretches between State Highway 78 and Business 78 and sits on the North side of FM 981.

Burial Information

Burial plots are available. Contact information is available below for availability and guidelines.

Funding

The Blue Ridge Cemetery is not owned or funded by the city. It is maintained by the generous funding of people in the community.

Once a year on the first Sunday in May, the cemetery association meets to elect board members, discuss any new or old business, as well as view the financial information for the cemetery. That weekend is Decoration Weekend.

Starting in 2025, First Baptist Church, located less than a quarter mile from the cemetery, has graciously agreed to accept online donations on behalf of the cemetery. This service allows for convenient, cost-free online giving. You can donate online at firstblueridge.org/give.

"We are happy to help the cemetery by offering our website as a portal for online giving. We are already setup and those donations can now be marked specifically for the cemetery in our giving portal. In fact, it is a privilege for us, as so many of our former members have been laid to rest on that very hill. -Pastor Shawn Bailey"
