About the Cemetery

Blue Ridge Cemetery is a peaceful and historic resting place nestled in the heart of Blue Ridge, Texas. It lies between State Highway 78, Business 78 and FM 981. With roots going back generations, the grounds are maintained with care and reverence by a board of directors and members of the Blue Ridge community.

Location

Located just outside Blue Ridge, TX, off County Road 123. Open to visitors during daylight hours.

Burial Information

Burial plots may be available. Contact the cemetery administrator for more information on availability and guidelines.
