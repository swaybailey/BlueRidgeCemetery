Woodbridge Cemetery

Blue Ridge, Texas

About the Cemetery

Woodbridge Cemetery is a peaceful and historic resting place nestled in the countryside of Blue Ridge, Texas. With roots going back to the 1800s, the grounds are maintained with care and reverence by the local community.

Location

Located off County Road 123, just minutes from downtown Blue Ridge, TX. Easily accessible and open to visitors during daylight hours.

Burial Information

Burial plots are available upon request. Please contact the cemetery board or local city office for more information on availability, policies, and services.
